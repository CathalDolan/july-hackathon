We Love Fresh Coffee

We are an independent artisan coffee roaster and cafe located in the heart of Cork. We love fresh coffee which we offer wholesale or by the bag. Why not come in and try our signature coffee and enjoy a slice of something sweet from our tasty cake selection.

Roastery

We roast in small quantities allowing us to offer truly fresh coffee to you. Using gentle roasting techniques, taking more time and care, ensuring flavours and aromas remain intact. We are transparent in our sourcing, allowing us to gain the providence from the farm to our green bean merchant to us. Farmers are rewarded with good prices for quality coffee, making the supply chain a truly sustainable model.

Location

A friendly and relaxed atmosphere with plenty of seating inside and a courtyard outside to enjoy a brew in the sunshine. It is the ideal place to come and check out our full range of retail coffee beans – available to take home.
